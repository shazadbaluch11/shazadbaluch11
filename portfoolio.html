About Me

Hello everybody, my name is Shahzad Rashid and I am an archaeologist. I am passionate about uncovering the past and learning about the cultures that have come before us. I have been working as an archaeologist for the past 10 years, and I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, including excavations at ancient sites in Egypt, Greece, and Turkey. I am always looking for new ways to learn and grow as an archaeologist, and I am excited to see what the future holds for my career.

Projects

Excavation of an ancient city in Egypt

I would love to excavate an ancient city in Egypt and learn more about the people who lived there. I am particularly interested in learning about the city's architecture, its economy, and its culture.

Survey of an archaeological landscape in Greece

I would also like to survey an archaeological landscape in Greece and identify potential sites for excavation. I am particularly interested in learning about the relationship between ancient settlements and the surrounding environment.

Analysis of artifacts from a shipwreck

I am interested in analyzing artifacts from a shipwreck to learn more about the people who traveled on the ship and the goods that they were trading. I am particularly interested in learning about the ship's construction, its cargo, and its destination.

Interpretation of archaeological data from a cave

I am interested in interpreting archaeological data from a cave to learn more about the people who lived in the cave and the activities that they carried out there. I am particularly interested in learning about the cave's environment, its inhabitants, and its artifacts.

Education and outreach about archaeology

I am also interested in educating the public about archaeology and its importance. I would like to develop educational programs for students and the public, give public talks, and write articles about archaeology. I believe that it is important to share the findings of archaeological research with the public so that everyone can learn about the past.
